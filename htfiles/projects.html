We care, and you should too.

Our water is being affected by climate change, which causes more extreme weather and unconsiderable living temperatures, which is increasing demand and pollution. We need to conserve water because it is a finite resource that is becoming less predictable and more polluted, threatening human health, ecosystems, and future generations.

Our Projects:

  • Data logging
  • Waste Water treatment
  • Public awareness campaigns

Water is a limited resource. What each of us does in the world, how we live, does make a difference. As we learn the value of clean, safe water and how scarce it truly is, we can take steps to protect it and to get it to people who lack access today.
