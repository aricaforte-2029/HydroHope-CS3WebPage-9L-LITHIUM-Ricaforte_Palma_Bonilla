WHY WE TAKE ACTION:

Protecting Ecosystems

Conserving water helps ecosystems by preserving habitats for aquatic life, reducing the energy needed for water treatment and transport which cuts down on carbon emissions, and mitigating drought impacts by maintaining groundwater levels.

Water Quality

Conserving water improves water quality by reducing the amount of wastewater that needs to be treated, which lessens the load on municipal sewer systems and wastewater treatment plants.

Supporting Agriculture

Conserving water improves agriculture by improving crop yields, increasing profitability, and promoting sustainability. It lowers operational costs, reduces strain on water resources, and helps protect ecosystems.

Improving Public Health

Conserving water improves public health by reducing the spread of waterborne diseases, improving overall hygiene and sanitation, and ensuring there is enough safe water for communities.
